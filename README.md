# Hinglish Audio Transcriber 🎙️✨

Hinglish Audio Transcriber is a highly-focused, zero-friction, single-screen web application optimized to process and analyze conversational speech in Hinglish (the natural blend of Hindi/Urdu and English spoken casually across South Asia).

While traditional transcription engines only recognize standard English or formal native scripts (Devanagari/Urdu), this tool bridges the gap by instantly converting casual spoken phrases into accurate, Romanized phonetic text.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

* **🎙️ Audio Upload & Direct Recording:** Upload your existing audio files (`.mp3`, `.wav`, `.m4a`) or record real-time voice directly inside your browser with a single click.
* **🎯 Hinglish Phonetics Transcription:** Powered by a customized speech recognition pipeline that understands regional accents, slangs, and conversational speech patterns to transcribe them in their actual Romanized phonetic form (e.g., *"kya haal hai"*).
* **🌎 Parallel Translations & Summaries:** 
  * **English Translation:** Instantly generates a parallel, grammatically accurate English translation alongside your transcription.
  * **AI Summary:** Synthesizes the conversation into a short, structured key-points summary to save you time.
* **🔍 Interactive Glossary & Search:** Filter and locate key terms or phrases instantly within your transcribed output using an interactive search bar.
* **⚡ Zero-Friction UX:** Completely free of subscription walls, SaaS billing screens, or tedious sign-up barriers. It is a clean, single-screen utility ready to work the second you open it.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** React.js, TypeScript (for robust type safety and speed)
* **Styling:** Tailwind CSS (modern responsive grid and sleek glassmorphic layouts)
* **Database & Config:** Firebase App Integration
* **Backend Runtime:** Node.js (Express) via `server.ts`
* **Build Tool:** Vite
